About HerAILife

HerAILife.com was built for women who are already doing it all — and are ready to let AI help carry some of the weight.

The AI revolution is happening right now. But most of the content out there is written for 20-something developers and tech executives. Not for women managing careers, families, relationships, health, and their own reinvention — all at the same time.

That gap felt wrong. So we built HerAILife — a free weekly newsletter that translates AI into plain English and shows you exactly how to use it in your actual life. No jargon. No hype. No homework.

Every issue covers one real topic — career, health, family, money, travel, relationships — and gives you a practical tip and the exact AI prompt to use it that week.

We believe women 35–60 are the most resourceful, adaptable humans on the planet. You just need tools that speak your language.
